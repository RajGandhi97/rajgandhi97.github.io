About

I am a first-year Ph.D. student studying pure mathematics at Cornell University. In 2021, I recieved a M.Sc. degree in mathematics from the Univerity of Ottawa. My M.Sc. advisors were Alistair Savage and Kirill Zainoulline. In 2019, I received a B.Sc. in physics-mathematics from the University of Ottawa.
